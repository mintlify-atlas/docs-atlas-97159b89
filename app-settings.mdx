title: "Application Settings"

---

Application settings let you personalise the look and language of Appium Inspector. They are
accessible from any screen in the application.

## Accessing settings

Click the **settings icon** (gear/cog) in the top-right corner of the Session Builder screen to
open the App Settings modal.

## Theme

Appium Inspector supports three theme modes:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Light Theme** | Always use the light colour scheme. |
| **Dark Theme** | Always use the dark colour scheme. |
| **System Theme** | Automatically follow the operating system's light/dark preference. |

By default, the Inspector follows the system theme. Click any of the three buttons in the Theme
section to switch modes immediately — no restart is required.
